# Privacy Policy

**◈ GhostLink Network**
*Last Updated: March 2026 · Applies to All GhostLink Services*

---

> **TL;DR:** GhostLink is designed with privacy in mind. We do not log your browsing activity through the proxy. The only data we collect is minimal anonymous usage data (total visit counts) via Supabase to understand traffic volume. All user settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser — never on our servers.

---

## 01 / Overview

GhostLink ("we," "our," or "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your information when you use GhostLink, including its web proxy, games hub, AI terminal, custom apps, and app marketplace features.

## 03 / Anonymous Analytics (Supabase)

GhostLink uses **Supabase** to count the total number of site visits. This counter is anonymous and does not associate any visit with an individual user, IP address, or session. The sole purpose of this data is to give the development team a rough understanding of site traffic volume.

No personal information is transmitted to Supabase in connection with this visit counter. Supabase's own privacy practices are governed by the [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

---

## 04 / Local Storage

GhostLink stores all user preferences, settings, themes, installed custom apps, keyboard shortcuts, and configuration data in your browser's **localStorage**. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any GhostLink server. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for the GhostLink domain.

Examples of data stored locally include:

- Theme preferences (colors, fonts, background images)
- Installed custom apps and their configurations
- Keyboard shortcut bindings
- Sidebar layout and pinned items
- Homescreen and clock settings
- Cloaking and panic key preferences

---

## 05 / Web Proxy — No Logging

GhostLink's web proxy (powered by ScramJet and BareMux, open-source technologies) is structured to not log, record, store, or inspect your browsing activity. Traffic routed through the proxy is not monitored or retained by GhostLink.

**However:** third-party websites you access through the proxy are governed by their own privacy policies and may collect data from you independently. GhostLink has no control over and takes no responsibility for third-party data collection practices.

---

## 06 / Third-Party Content & External Links

GhostLink loads certain resources from third-party CDNs (including jsDelivr and Cloudflare) and embeds or links to third-party games, tools, and content. These third-party services may have their own data collection practices. GhostLink does not control and is not responsible for any data collected by these third parties.

Third-party services referenced in GhostLink include:

- **jsDelivr CDN** — for serving JavaScript libraries and hosted content
- **Supabase** — for anonymous visit counting
- **GitHub** — for hosting GhostLink's source code and legal documents
- **Discord** — for community access (external, governed by Discord's privacy policy)

---

## 07 / Cookies

GhostLink does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Any cookies that may be set are strictly functional (e.g., session persistence for proxied pages) and do not identify you personally. GhostLink does not use third-party advertising cookies.

---

## 08 / Data Security

Since GhostLink does not collect or store personal data on its servers, the risk of a server-side data breach affecting your personal information is minimal. All user-facing data is stored in your own browser and is subject to your browser's and device's security measures.

---

## 09 / Your Rights & Choices

Because GhostLink does not store personal data server-side, most traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) are self-managed:

- **Clear your data:** Delete GhostLink's localStorage and cached data through your browser settings
- **Opt out of analytics:** You may block Supabase analytics requests using a browser extension such as uBlock Origin
- **Contact us:** For any privacy concerns, reach out via [Discord](https://discord.gg/dKs2sUNUXd)
